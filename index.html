My research interests are data management, cloud computing, machine learning and information retrieval.

I am Senior Researcher and Project Manager at the Mobile Soultions area of ISMB. I obtained my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the Univerisy of Turin where I defended my dissertation about the topic of "Extraction of semantically relevant fragments from television news textual transcriptions" as joint collaboration between Università di TOrino and Centro Ricerche e Innovazione Tecnologica RAI (CRIT-RAI).

I've participated in numerous National and European projects in the areas of big data, cloud computing, mobile sensing and location-based services.

I am ISMB representative for the Big Data Value Association (BDVA), an industry-led contractual counterpart to the European Commission for the implementation of the Big Data Value Public Private Partnership (BDV PPP) and to boost European BIG DATA VALUE research.

A detailed lists of appointments is available in my resume.

Research projects

713788

MUV - Mobility Urban Values (EU H2020, No. 723521-2)
Principal investigator of the data architecture aiming at increasing the citizen awareness on the quality of the urban environment leveraging crowdsourcing, big data and cloud computing technologies.

OptiTruck - Optimal fuel consumption with Predictive PowerTrain control and calibration for intelligent Truck (EU H2020, No. 713788)
Leader of Work Package 3 “Cloud computing architecture and system design” aiming at designing and developing a global optimizer based on cloud computing and Big Data for effective powertrain management, integrating heterogeneous data from transport mission, road topography, weather and road conditions and surrounding vehicles.

I-REACT - Improving Resilience to Emergencies through Advanced Cyber Technologies
Support to project management and coordination.
Privacy and Security Manager.
Leader of Work Package 5 “Service Oriented Architecture” aiming at increasing the resilience of citizens and assets to natural disasters through the use of mobile data, geospatial data and social network sentiment analysis.

Intellego
Technical Manager and leader of a proof of the concept for the valorisation of the big amount of data generated from the National Education Evaluation Platform (Sistema Nazionale di Valutazione - MIUR) in order to apply Data Analytics techniques for the evaluation of the performances of the national education system.

AIScinT - Automatic Ionospheric Scintillation deTector
Machine learning algorithms application (decision trees, random forest, support vector machines and neural networks) and models implementation for the early detection of atmospheric scintillation events occurring at equatorial and Antarctic regions to reduce the impact on GPS navigation systems.

GHOST - Galileo EnHancement as BoOster of the Smart CiTies
Leader of Work Package “Needs analysis and system requirements”. Management of technical activities related to the design of a mobile application aiming at collection data from the urban environment in order to increase the citizens awareness and participation.

FLOODIS - Floods management and forecast service
Support to project management and coordination. Design and development of an cloud-based positioning module leveraging European space assets (EGNOS/EDAS).

iDea - Innovative Diesel Engine Application
User needs and requirements analysis and architectural design of a cloud based service for the fruition of an advanced location-based system from innovative vehicles on-board units.
